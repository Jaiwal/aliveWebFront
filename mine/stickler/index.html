Bhagat Singh also known as Shaheed Bhagat Singh (28 September 1907 – 23 March 1931) was an Indian socialist and a revolutionary. He is considered to be one of the most influential revolutionaries of the Indian Independence Movement. He was born to Vidyavati and Sardar Kisan Singh Sandhu,in a Sikh jat family on 27 September 1907 in lyaalpur,Punjab in British India
